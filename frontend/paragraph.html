1) NSUT

Netaji Subhas University of Technology, formerly Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology is a state university located in Dwarka, New Delhi, India.
In 2018, the institute was granted University status, changing its name to Netaji Subhas University of Technology.

2) DTU

Delhi Technological University, formerly known as the Delhi College of Engineering is a state university in Rohini, Delhi, India.
It was established in 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic. In 1952, it started giving degrees after being affiliated with the University of Delhi. Wikipedia Address: P4X9+Q8X, Bawana Rd, Delhi Technological University, Shahbad Daulatpur Village, Rohini, New Delhi, Delhi 110042

3) IGDTUW

Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women, is an all women's university located in New Delhi, India on the heritage campus at Kashmere Gate, Delhi.
It was founded as the Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology in 1998. Wikipedia Address: James Church, New Church Rd, Opp. St, Kashmere Gate, New Delhi, Delhi 110006 Phone: 011 2390 0220
